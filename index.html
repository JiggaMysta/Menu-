Butternut Soup

Subscribe to our newsletter

Ingredients

  • 1 Onion, chopped
  • 15ml Butter
  • 15ml Crushed garlic
  • 2 Carrots, cleaned and diced
  • 15ml Dried chille flakes
  • 1 Butternut (about 2.5kg), cleaned and cubed
  • Item11.5L Vegetable stock
  • 125ml Fresh cream
  • Salt and peppper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a pen, saute the onion and garlic in butter till soft, but not brown.
  2. Add the carrots and saute for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Then add the chille flakes, a pinch of salt and pepper and saute for a further minute
  4. Next, add the vegetable stock. Bring to boil, then reduce the heat and let the heat and let simmer for 25 minutes.
  5. Let the soup cool slightly and use an immersesion blender to blend until smooth.
  6. Finally, add the cream and stir though.

Note: For added protein, add a cup of butter beans reight before blending.

Butternut: High in Vitamin A

One cup of cooked butternut squash provides more than 450% of the recommended daily intake (RDI) of vitamin A. This vitamin is essential for regulating cell growth, eye health, bone health and immune function.
